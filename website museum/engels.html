Who are we?
Our history

A garage box in The Hague is the place where the adventure started in 2008, with two friends Hasan Tasdemir and Pascal Rappailles. The garage quickly became too small, after which they moved to a shed in an industrial area. Unfortunately, the shed was not heated and everything had to be done in the middle of winter transferred to an empty Ministry of Agriculture. When this building also had to be emptied the then collection of 10 arcade video games with some spare parts was stored in an old showroom for classic cars. Because there was no room to renovate the cabinets or suitable space was looked for to play. It was found in 2011 at Cobaltstraat Zoetermeer.

What can you see?

As soon as you enter the museum, you are showered with everything that makes the game culture so beautiful and makes impressive. Follow our timeline through the atmosphere rooms and discover the history of video games on the basis of the game changers, highlights and important moments in technique, artwork and culture within the gaming industry. Play classics, meet icons and discover forgotten treasures of the video games. The museum has around 231 game computers, home computers and arcade cabinets ready to play stand, and 327 unique play areas. That makes us the museum with the largest arcade in all of Europe!

Activities

Our Lab is a dynamic space. From admiring game art to traveling through new ones worlds. There is always something new to experience in the Lab: one time you will learn all about the history of gaming during a lecture and the other time we welcome you during one tournament or a meet-up. Experience the future of gaming, or shape it yourself in the Lab! in our Barcade, where modern catering and retro arcade cabinets come together in a cozy environment. Here you can refuel after hours of adventures, challenges and saving the world. In addition to the well-known halal hot dogs and sandwiches we now also offer healthy sandwiches.
